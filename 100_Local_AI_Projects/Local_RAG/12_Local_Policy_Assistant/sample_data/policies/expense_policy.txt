Expense Reimbursement Policy — Effective January 2025

Section 1: Eligible Expenses
Business travel, client meals, conference registration fees, and work-related software subscriptions are eligible for reimbursement.

Section 2: Spending Limits
Meals: $50/person for client dinners, $25 for solo business meals. Hotels: Up to $250/night in major metros (NYC, SF, LA), $175 elsewhere. Flights: Economy class for domestic. Business class for international 6+ hours.

Section 3: Submission Process
Submit all expenses within 30 calendar days via Concur. Receipts required for all expenses over $25. Late submissions (over 60 days) will be denied.

Section 4: Approval Hierarchy
Under $500: Manager approval. $500-$5,000: Director approval. Over $5,000: VP approval. International travel requires pre-approval.

Section 5: Non-Reimbursable Items
Personal meals, alcohol (unless client entertainment), personal travel extensions, premium economy upgrades (domestic), gym memberships.
